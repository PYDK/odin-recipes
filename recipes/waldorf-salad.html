Turkey Carcass Soup

Description

Delicious salad that includes protein and a mix of vegetables.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 3 apples -- peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • ½ cup raisins (Optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk together mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, and salt in a serving bowl.
  2. Stir in apples, celery, walnuts, and raisins. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
